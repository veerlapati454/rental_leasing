What makes the process different.

No purchase order chains, no separate maintenance vendor, no guessing at rates. These four things hold true across every lease, regardless of category or term length.

Step 02, closer look

What "inspected and fueled" actually covers.

Every asset passes the same pre-delivery checklist before it leaves the depot, regardless of category. Nothing ships that wouldn't pass it.

  • Full mechanical and safety inspection within 24 hours of dispatch
  • Fluids, tires, and fuel topped off before loading
  • Digital condition report photographed and attached to your account
  • On-site or curbside walkthrough at drop-off, no extra charge

Step 03, closer look

One call, not a maintenance contract to manage.

Report an issue from the dashboard or by phone. A technician is dispatched from the nearest of 178 depots, and if the fix can't happen on-site same-day, a replacement unit is queued automatically — no separate approval needed.

  • Scheduled servicing pre-booked around your usage hours or mileage
  • Breakdown coverage included in every lease, no add-on fee
  • Replacement units dispatched same-day when repair can't happen on-site
  • Full service history logged to your account automatically
Step 04, closer look

Renewing takes fewer clicks than the first reservation.

Every active and past lease lives in one dashboard. As a term approaches its end, extend it, swap to a different unit if the scope of work changed, or schedule a pickup — without starting a new contract from zero.

  • Renewal reminders sent automatically before term end
  • Swap requests routed to the nearest depot with matching inventory
  • Pickup scheduling handled from the same screen as the reservation
  • Invoices, condition reports, and service history archived per asset
