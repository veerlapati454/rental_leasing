Section 05

How we grade condition.

Every asset is graded at intake and re-graded after each lease, so the listing you see reflects the unit you'll actually receive.

A

Showroom Ready

Under 500 hours or 10,000 miles. Cosmetically near-new, full service history, no outstanding wear items.

≈ 22% of active fleet
B

Field Proven

Regularly serviced, light cosmetic wear consistent with normal use, mechanically inspected before every lease.

≈ 61% of active fleet
C

Budget Tier

Higher hours or mileage, visible cosmetic wear, fully functional and safety-certified at a reduced day rate.

≈ 17% of active fleet
